About Us

Hello my name is Aaron Simeone. I am 25 years old and I am the Founder and CEO of "Used and Re-Use". I started this company out of Alliance, Ohio back in 2020 during the Covid-19 outbreak.

My mission is to make sure good quality used items that people have are not wasted and thrown away. Instead I buy them up and then sell them for cheaper prices to those who are in need. I follow the old saying of "Ones mans trash is another mans treasure." Doing this keeps items that are perfectly fine and working out of landfills or being horded in someones basement.

I hope that I can inspire other out there to do the same with their used items and instead throwing them away or forgetting about them, they can be Used and Re-Use.
